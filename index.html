Homemade and homegrown

Grown in the quiet, cozy corner of northwest Missouri, Gibbees Homemade Honey produces small-batch artisan honey in a lovingly and holistic manner. A literal mom-and-pop operation where Jack harvests the honey and Debbie filters, jars, decorates, and avoids their worker bee's stings.
