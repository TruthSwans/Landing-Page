Homemade and homegrown

Grown in the quiet, cozy corner of northwest Missouri, GibBees Backyard Honey produces small-batch artisan honey in a lovingly and holistic manner. A literal mom-and-pop operation that begas as a 4-H project for Josiah. Jack harvests the honey and Debbie jars, decorates, and avoids their worker bees' stings.
